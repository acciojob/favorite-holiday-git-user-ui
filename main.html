Paris, France


Paris is one of the most beautiful cities in the world, known for its rich history, stunning architecture, romantic atmosphere, and world-class art museums. It attracts millions of visitors every year.

  1. Eiffel Tower – The most iconic landmark of Paris
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to the famous Mona Lisa
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A masterpiece of Gothic architecture
  4. Champs-Élysées – The most famous avenue in the city
  5. Montmartre – Known for its artistic history and scenic views
  • Croissants – Flaky and buttery breakfast pastry
  • Crêpes – Sweet or savory thin pancakes
  • Macarons – Colorful French sandwich cookies
  • Ratatouille – Traditional vegetable dish
  • Baguette – Classic French bread
